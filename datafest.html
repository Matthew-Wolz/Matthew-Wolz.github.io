DataFest Achievements

Member of Truman State University's team that won Best in Show at the American Statistical Association DataFest (April 5-6, 2025), hosted by the University of Missouri. Our team of four analyzed a large dataset from Savills, a commercial real estate advising firm, to identify overpriced or underpriced property categories. We created an executive summary and a five-minute presentation with statistical analysis and visualizations in under 24 hours.

Team: Matthew Wolz, Jossua Chartier, Bek Usmonov, Nathan Bresette
